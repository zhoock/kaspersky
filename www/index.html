About

We believe, that knowledge is power. A power to strive harder, perform better and win every race. This power needs to be saved. That is why Kaspersky Lab provides Ferrari with full IT security and fights a high-tech battle against all potential threads. Kaspersky has tailored an endpoint solution that meets all Ferrari’s specific needs.

Make your knowledge your power, your speed and your acceleration! Turn the information into the fuel. Get your own Kaspersky security to protect it, so that one day you will find yourself at the real racing tribune.
